Shipping Information

Delivery Options

Standard Shipping — $9.99 · Estimated 3–7 business days

Express Shipping — $14.99 · Estimated 1–3 business days

Free Standard Shipping — on all orders over $100, applied automatically at checkout.

Estimated Delivery by State

NSW / VIC / ACT: 2–4 business days (standard), next business day (express)

QLD / SA / WA / NT / TAS: 4–7 business days (standard), 2–3 business days (express)

Remote areas may require an additional 1–2 business days. Orders placed before 12 pm AEST on business days are dispatched the same day.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched you'll receive a shipping confirmation email with your tracking number.

You can track your parcel at any time via the Australia Post website using that tracking number. If you haven't received a dispatch email within 2 business days of your order, please check your spam folder or contact us.

International Shipping

We currently ship to New Zealand, United Kingdom, United States, and Canada.

New Zealand — $19.99 · 5–10 business days

UK / US / Canada — $29.99 · 10–15 business days

International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes levied by the destination country. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient.

Missed Delivery & Undeliverable Parcels

If no one is home at the time of delivery, Australia Post will leave a card with instructions to collect your parcel from the nearest post office or arrange redelivery.

Parcels uncollected after 10 business days will be returned to us. We'll contact you to arrange reshipment — additional shipping fees may apply.

Returns & Refunds

Our Promise

We want you to love everything you receive from ThreadCo. If something isn't right, we make the return process as simple and stress-free as possible.

How to Start a Return

Step 1. Email returns@threadco.com.au with your order number and reason for your return.

Step 2. We'll send you a prepaid return label within 1 business day.

Step 3. Pack your item securely in its original packaging and drop it off at any Australia Post location.

Step 4. Once we receive and inspect the item, we'll process your refund within 3–5 business days.

Return Conditions

Returns are accepted within 30 days of the delivery date.

To be eligible, items must be unworn and unwashed, in their original condition with all tags still attached, and free from perfume, make-up, or any marks.

Swimwear and underwear are non-returnable for hygiene reasons. Items marked as Final Sale are not eligible for returns.

Refunds

Refunds are issued to your original payment method:

Credit / debit card: 3–5 business days after approval.

PayPal: 1–3 business days after approval.

You'll receive an email confirmation once your refund is processed. If you haven't received it after 7 business days, please contact us.

Exchanges

We don't currently offer direct exchanges. To swap for a different size or colour, simply return your original item and place a new order — it's quicker and guarantees your preferred item is reserved.

Need help finding the right size? Visit our Size Guide or contact our team.
